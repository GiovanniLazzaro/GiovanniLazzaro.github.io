I am a **Clinical Neurophysiology Technician (NTP)** and **PhD student in Neuroscience** at the University of Padova (UniPD). I work as a Research Assistant at [IRCCS San Camillo Hospital](https://hsancamillo.it) in Venice, Italy, where I am part of the [ONDA Lab](https://sancamilloirccs-ondalab.github.io/) (Open methodologies in Neurorehabilitation and Data Analysis).

My work sits at the intersection of clinical neurophysiology, cognitive neuroscience, and open science. When I'm not struggling with EEG data or surviving my PhD, I'm dreaming of a tiny house in the woods.

## Research Interests

- **Neurophysiology** — LFP and EEG/MEG signal processing and analysis
- **Cognitive and Clinical Neuroscience** — Focus on Beta Burst and Parkinson's Disease
- **Neuromodulation** — non-invasive brain stimulation techniques (TMS, tDCS, tACS)
- **Clinical Applications of Brain Stimulation**
